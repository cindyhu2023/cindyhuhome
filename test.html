About Me.

Hi I’m Cindy! Nice to meet you!

I’m from Taipei, Taiwan, and now I’m a sophomore pursing dual bachelor’s degrees in computer science and communication studies at Northwestern University. I’m very interested in web dev, UI/UX design, human-computer interaction, and research! (probably will add more in the future -- there are just too many fun stuff to learn.)

Outside of classrooms, I am the operations chair for DISC NU, where we worked closely with local non-profit orgs to create social impacts with techonology. I’m also a research assistant at Delta Lab, and I’m so glad I join this fun & supporting research community!
